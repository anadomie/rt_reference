Combat Circumstances

Circumstance Description
Cover Cover increases your effective AP(Better cover = more AP). Cover AP can be decreased after being hit. Covers certain body parts. Usually legs and body.
Darkness -20 to WS, -30 to BS. +20 to Stealth
Difficult Terrain Can reduce Movement (and movement related actions). Certain terrain may also reduce WS.
Engaged in Melee -20 BS to targets engaged in melee Combat.
Extreme Range Targets at a distance of more than 3x the range of a weapon are considered to be at Extreme Range. Ballistic Skill tests to hit targets at Extreme Range suffer a -30 penalty.
Fog, Mist, Shadow or Smoke +10 to Stealth tests. -20 to BS
Ganging Up Melee attackers gain a bonus when outnumbering a target. +10WS for each additional ally engaged in melee combat.
Helpless Targets Melee attacks against unconscious or otherwise Helpless targets automatically succeed. The Degrees of Success are the same as the attacker's Weapon Skill bonus. When rolling for damage, roll twice and add the two results. Prone and Stunned targets are not automatically considered helpless.
High Ground Characters standing on higher ground gain a +10 bonus to Weapon Skill.
Long Range Targets at a distance of more than 2x the range of a weapon are considered to be at Long Range. Ballistic Skill tests to hit targets at Long Range suffer a -10 penalty.
Long Range While pinned you may only take a half action. -20 to BS. You must use your turn to seek cover or move away from the attacker if none is available. At the end of the turn make a WP Test (+30 if in cover) to overcome being pinned.
Point-Blank Range Targets at a distance of 2 metres or closer are considered to be at Point-Blank Range. Ballistic Skill tests to hit Point-Blank targets gain a +30 modifier. This modifier is lost if the attacker is engaged in melee combat with the target. For weapons with a Short Range of less than 3 metres, Point-Blank is considered to be 1 metre instead.
Prone A character is considered Prone any time he is lying on the ground. Weapon Skill tests gain +10 bonus to hit Prone characters, but Ballistic Skill tests suffer a -10 penalty. A character who is Prone suffers a -10 penalty to Weapon Skill tests, and a -20 penalty to Evasion tests.
Short Range Targets at a distance of less than half the range of a weapon are considered to be at Short Range. Ballistic Skill tests to hit targets at Short Range gain a +10 bonus.
Stunned Targets Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill tests to hit Stunned targets gain a +20 bonus.
Unaware Targets Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill tests to hit Unaware targets gain a +30 bonus.
Weather and Unnatural Conditions Certain weather or other unnatural conditions may cause penalties to attacks.

Special Conditions

Condition Description
Amputated Limbs When a character loses a limb he also suffers Blood Loss and must be treaded by someone with the Medicae skill. If not treated roll 1d10. on 9+ you survive, else you die from an infection ofe rthe next 1d10 days. No natural healing for 10+2 days.
Blinded Automatically fail all vision related tests (including BS). -30 to WS or other tests that would benefit from vision.
Blood Loss Take 1 Fatigue every turn. Any character can attempt a free-10 Medicae Test to staunch the bleeding.
Deafened Automatically fail all hearing related tests. You can not communicate verbally.
Falling 1d10 + meters fallen - Toughness Bonus damage. Only apllies to heights within reason.
Fatigue A character's Fatigue Threshold is his Toughness Bonus + Willpower Bonus. If Fatigue passes this threshold you fall unconcious for 10-[Toughness Bonus] minutes. After waking up the fatigue level is set to the threshhold. If Fatigue reaches 2x the threshold you die. 6 hours of uninterrupted rest removes all Fatigue. Every hour of low activity also removes 1 level.
Fire Characters on fire suffer 1d10 - Toughness Bonus Energy damage and 1 fatigue each round. WP test in order to take any action. Attemt to extinguish by falling prone and making a -20 Agility Test.
Lost Arm/Hand -20 to all Tests requiring two hands. Unable to wield two-handed weapons.
Lost Eye Permanent -10 BS. -20 to vision related tests. If you lose both eyes your are blind.
Lost Foot All Movment reduced to half (rounded up). -20 to all movment actions and tests.
Lost Leg All Movment reduced to half (rounded up). -20 to all movment actions and tests. Unable to dodge.
Stunned Unable to use Actions or Reactions.
Suffocation You can hold your breath for [Toughness Bonus] minutes. In combat this is reduced to 2x [Toughness Bonus] rounds. Each Minute/Round Toughness test or get 1 level of fatigue. You fall unconscious after holding your breath for the maximum duration and die if you are deprived of oxygen for another [Toughness Bonus] rouinds.
Unconsciousness Can not take actions or reactions. Considered as a Helpless Target. If no specific duration is given, Unconsciousness defaults to 10 minutes.
