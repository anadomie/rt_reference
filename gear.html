Drug Kick A combat drug. Removes Fatigue and grants immunity to it for 2d10 rounds. Once it wears off, take 1d5 Fatigue.
Drug Leatherwort A drug that is rubbed into the skin to make it tougher and numb. User gains +2 Armour (stacks with actual armour) and +10 to Toughness tests.
Drug Lho-Sticks Space cigarettes.
Drug Obscura A recreational drug, often illegal. Enter a dreamlike state for 1d5 hours, after which you enter a deep depression for 1d10 hours unless another dose is taken.
Drug Rainbow A dangerous but catch-all drug. Grants a reroll to resist disease or toxins, and automatically stops blood loss. Must pass a Toughness test or take 1d5 unsoakable wounds.
Consumable Ration Pack Eating a ration pack while resting for at least 2 hours allows the user to recover an extra point of Fatigue.
Consumable Recaf Removes 1 Fatigue. Repeated doses have reduced effectiveness.
Consumable Sacred Unguents Requires a Full Action to apply. Makes a gun immune to jamming for the duration of one magazine. If already jammed, it unjams but does not gain this immunity.
Drug Sandstone A powerful drug that increases the user's resistance to pain, fear, and interrogation.
Drug Scav-Glysten Drinking it helps reduce the user's scent, making it difficult to track them by it, by both animals and men.
Drug Scraper-Ripper Grants +30 to all Agility tests for 1d5 hours, but the user must make a -20 WP test to avoid reacting violently if surprised.
Poison Silver Anathema A rare mechanical poison that spontaneously creates metal thorns within the victim, injuring them directly and often internally.
Drug Slam A combat drug. Grants Unnatural Strength (x2) and Unnatural Toughness (x2) for 1d5 rounds. Also permanently reduces Strength and Agility by 1d5.
Drug Slaught Increase Agility and Perception bonus by 3 for 2d10 minutes. Afterwards, test Toughness or take -20 to Agility and Perception tests for 1d5 hours. Also called onslaught.
Drug Snake Grants Unnatural Agility (1) and Unnatural Perception (2) for 1d5+5 rounds. Inflicts 10 Willpower damage and 1 Corruption when the drug wears off.
Drug Somna -10 Medicae to use properly. 4 Degrees of Failure results in death, success means death-like trance for 1d10 days. Subject may be revived with stimm, but must test Toughness to survive it.
Drug Spook Randomly induces psychic ability, possibly causing Insanity.
Drug Spur User is immune to Fatigue and Stunning for 2d10 minutes, then takes -20 to Toughness/Agility tests for the next hour and may be fatigued. If used 6 times in a week, -20 WP test or be addicted.
Drug Stimm Lasts 3d10 rounds, ignore characteristic penalties from damage and cannot be Stunned. When it wears off, take a -20 penalty to Strength, Toughness and Agility tests for one hour.
Drug Tranq A low-quality booze known for its numbing, depressive effect.
Drug White Void Gain +20 WP for 1d10 minutes per dose. If used more than twice a week, take -20 WP test or become addicted, requiring daily use.
Drug Corpse Obmulen User gains +20 Strength, but gains Fatigue and possibly 1d10 Strength damage for the next day.
Drug Shudderstep User gains Unnatural Speed, but gains Fatigue and possibly passes out for 1d10+5 minutes.
Drug Murder's Boon User gains Proven(4) on all melee attacks, but gains Fatigue and possibly -20 to Fellowship, Intelligence and Willpower for 1d5 hours.
Drug Eviscerine User gains +20 Weapon Skill, but gains Fatigue and possibly loses his Reaction for 1d10 hours.
Poison Essence of Perfect Vitriol Toxin. Test Toughness or suffer 1d10 Strength Damage +1 per Degree of Failure. Originally made by the Stryxis.
Poison Final Breath Toxin. Test Toughness or start suffocating.
Poison Heartfire Toxin. Test Toughness (-10) or suffer 1d10E damage to the body when exerting yourself or taking Impact or Explosive damage.
Poison Liquid Agony Toxin. Test Toughness or be stunned for 1 round per point of Perception Bonus.
Poison Nightmare Philtre Toxin. Test Toughness or count as having failed a fear test and roll on the Shock Table. Is ignored by fearless creatures and makes Orks angry.
Poison Sanguine Exodus Toxin. Test Toughness or suffer from blood loss.

Drug Enhanced Metabolism A Dark Eldar who imbibes this drug gains the Regeneration (4) and Undying traits.
Drug Eternal Hatred A Dark Eldar who imbibes this drug grants his melee attacks the Vengeful (7) quality.
